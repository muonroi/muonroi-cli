<objective>
Wire two integrations the loop needs from supporting subsystems:

1. **Cost scoping** — extend ledger to support per-product budget namespaces, with a separate JSONL store
   at `~/.muonroi/usage/products/<runId>.jsonl`. The new `commitToProduct` helper writes BOTH ledgers
   transactionally (one append after the monthly commit succeeds). Two-cap enforcement: halt on first hit.

2. **EE phase-tracker boundary wiring** — provide `postSprintBoundary` that the loop driver calls at
   each sprint transition. Explicitly calls `setPhase("sprint-N")` (RESEARCH §2 confirmed boundary triggers
   are setPhase calls, not iterations.md appends) and fires `phase-outcome` with the sprint verdict.
   Extend `PhaseOutcomeKind` to include `"aborted"` and `"resumed"` (RESEARCH §3.7 — preferred path).

Output:
- `src/usage/types.ts` edit (~+3 LoC) — add `productRunId?: string` to ReservationToken

- `src/product-loop/cost-scoper.ts` (NEW, ~120 LoC) — pre-flight two-cap check + reserve wrapper
- `src/product-loop/phase-tracker-bridge.ts` (NEW, ~70 LoC) — setPhase + outcome firing
